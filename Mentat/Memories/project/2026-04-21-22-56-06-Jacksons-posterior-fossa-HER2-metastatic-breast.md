# Memory

Jackson's posterior fossa HER2+ metastatic breast cancer radiotherapy paper is missing several high-priority references that directly support his study design and systemic therapy variables. Zare et al. (2026) network meta-analysis is HIGH PRIORITY for systemic therapy context (pyrotinib, lapatinib, TKI combinations). Phillips et al. (2024) TROG 16.02 is CRITICAL — Australian Phase II trial with ≤5 brain metastases treated with SRS/surgery, directly comparable to Jackson's three arms and provides local vs distant brain failure pattern data. Niikura et al. (2023) ROSET-BM trial documents T-DXd emergence as standard-of-care for HER2+ BCBM with 62.7% intracranial ORR and strong LMD outcomes. Amsbaugh et al. (2016) provides dosimetric support for margin definitions — marginal dose ≥24 Gy associated with significantly lower in-field failure, directly supporting Jackson's in-field vs marginal failure distinction.
