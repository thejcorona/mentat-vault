# Memory

Jackson's [[Lumra cEDH]] deck is restricted to green and colorless cards only. This color limitation eliminates cards like Weathered Wayfarer (white) and requires careful tutor/untapper selection from available green/colorless pieces: Expedition Map, Crop Rotation, Summoner's Pact, Green Sun's Zenith, Elvish Reclaimer, Natural Order, Chord of Calling, Tempt with Discovery for tutoring; Magus of the Candelabra, Krosan Restorer, Deserted Temple, Arid Archway, Guildless Commons for untapping/bouncing. This constraint shapes which combo lines are actually viable in the deck.
