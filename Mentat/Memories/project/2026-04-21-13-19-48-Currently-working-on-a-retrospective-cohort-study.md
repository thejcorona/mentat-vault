# Memory

Currently working on a retrospective cohort study of HER2+ metastatic breast cancer patients with intracranial metastases treated with radiotherapy. The study compares three treatment arms: SRS (any location), PFRT, and SRS to posterior fossa specifically. Primary focus is failure patterns in posterior fossa HER2+ brain mets — investigating whether in-field, marginal, distant intracranial, or leptomeningeal failures differ by treatment modality.
