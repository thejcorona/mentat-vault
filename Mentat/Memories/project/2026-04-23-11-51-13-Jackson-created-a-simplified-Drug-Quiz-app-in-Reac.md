# Memory

Jackson created a simplified Drug Quiz app in [[React]] with 100 essential hospital drugs (cardiovascular, antibiotics, analgesics, respiratory, endocrine, psychiatry/neurology, GI, miscellaneous). App includes three modes: flashcards (flip-card with progress tracking), use case quiz (multiple choice), and dose quiz (multiple choice with scoring). Built to prevent timeout issues with earlier version. Designed as study/exam reference for final year medical school.
