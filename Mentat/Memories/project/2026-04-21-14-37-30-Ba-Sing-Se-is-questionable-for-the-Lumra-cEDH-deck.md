# Memory

Ba Sing Se is questionable for the [[Lumra]] cEDH deck. Text reads: enters tapped unless you control a basic land, taps for {G}, has sorcery-speed Earthbend ability for {2}{G}. Earthbend is irrelevant in cEDH. Essentially a basic-land-conditional untapped green source with no meaningful upside. In a deck that often has no basics in play, it frequently enters tapped. Marked for potential removal.
