# Memory

Jackson has specific methodological challenges with the posterior fossa radiotherapy paper: needs prespecified margin definition to distinguish marginal from in-field failure after SRS; leptomeningeal disease (LMD) exclusion at baseline is appropriate but LMD development as secondary outcome may overlap with distant intracranial failure in posterior fossa context and requires tight radiological definition upfront. Statistical approach for handling confounders (LMD, KPS/ECOG) not yet specified but should follow foundational verification work.
