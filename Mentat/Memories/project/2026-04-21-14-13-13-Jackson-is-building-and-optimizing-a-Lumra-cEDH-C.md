# Memory

Jackson is building and optimizing a [[Lumra]] [[cEDH]] (Competitive Elder Dragon Highlander) deck. Lumra is a mana dork commander with a card advantage engine off her activated ability (tapping lands to draw and ramp), rewarding high density of mana rocks and payoff pieces. The deck runs multiple sacrifice outlets (Zuran Orb, Sylvan Safekeeper, Strip Mine, Wasteland, Lotus Field), ETB mana generators (Lotus Cobra, Tiller Engine, Amulet of Vigor, Spelunking, Nissa Resurgent Animist), and win lines including Sunscorched Desert damage loops, Field of the Dead zombie army, and Finale of Devastation. Core combo features Ashaya + Vesuva copying Lumra (a land under Ashaya) for instant-speed loops via Emergence Zone. Deck achieves 16.26% conversion rate on EDHTop16, placing Lumra in tier 2 cEDH territory. Jackson's current list has strong acceleration and stax disruption (Six, Vexing Bauble, Warping Wail, Dosan, Heartwood Storyteller) but includes several cards Jackson wants to interrogate: Famished Worldsire, Icetill Explorer, Horizon Explorer, Tezzeret Cruel Captain, Nature's Rhythm, Ba Sing Se, Fal'Cie Paradise, Balamb Garden, Formidable Speaker, Insidious Fungus, and Chord of Calling. Sideboard contains strong candidates to move into main deck: Gaea's Cradle, Earthcraft (Ashaya combo), Mox Diamond (speed), The Tabernacle at Pendrell Vale, Scapeshift, Life from the Loam, and Manabond. Jackson plans to workshop this list in depth with Mentat for optimization of lines of play, meta positioning, and flex slots.
