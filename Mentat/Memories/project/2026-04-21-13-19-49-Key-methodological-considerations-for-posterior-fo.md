# Memory

Key methodological considerations for posterior fossa radiotherapy paper: needs prespecified margin definition to distinguish marginal from in-field failure after SRS; leptomeningeal disease (LMD) exclusion at baseline is appropriate but LMD development as secondary outcome may overlap with distant intracranial failure in posterior fossa context — requires tight radiological definition upfront. Plan to handle confounders (LMD, KPS/ECOG) statistically but approach not yet specified.
