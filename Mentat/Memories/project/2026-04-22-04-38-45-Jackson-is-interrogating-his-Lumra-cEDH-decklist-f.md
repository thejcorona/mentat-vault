# Memory

Jackson is interrogating his [[Lumra cEDH]] decklist for unexplored combo variations by identifying tutor cards (Expedition Map, Crop Rotation, Summoner's Pact, Green Sun's Zenith) and lands with inherent untap/bounce mechanics (Deserted Temple, Arid Archway, Guildless Commons) that could create previously undocumented loops. His systematic approach: pull actual card mechanics, map them to established win conditions (Sunscorched Desert pinging, Field of the Dead zombie army, One Ring protection), then identify gaps in documented combo framework.
