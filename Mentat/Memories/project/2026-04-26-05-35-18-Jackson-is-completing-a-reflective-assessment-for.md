# Memory

Jackson is completing a reflective assessment for medical school using SOLO taxonomy Level 4/5 work. The framework has three parts: (1) describing a stimulus event/topic with clear logical sequencing, context, environment, non-verbal communication for 'bigger picture'; (2) reflecting on own responses while considering other perspectives (interwoven); (3) concluding with new ways of behaving or thinking in professional context. Assessment emphasizes self-analysis, insight, reference to literature, alternative solutions, comparison of perspectives, and synthesis leading to new understanding.
