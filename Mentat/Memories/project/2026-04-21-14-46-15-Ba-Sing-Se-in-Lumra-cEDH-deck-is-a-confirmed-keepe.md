# Memory

[[Ba Sing Se]] in [[Lumra cEDH]] deck is a confirmed keeper — the [[Earthbend]] ability functions as a legitimate combo piece, not just conditional ramp. Line 1: [[Ashaya]] + Lumra + untapper + sac outlet creates a full loop where Earthbend targets Lumra (a land under Ashaya), she becomes a 0/0 creature with 2 counters, dies to sac outlet, Earthbend trigger returns her tapped, Lumra's ETB fires, untapper untaps her, repeat infinitely without commander tax. Line 2: Earthbend a mana-producing land + [[Springheart Nantuko]] + [[Lotus Cobra]] generates infinite landfall triggers and mana. The sorcery-speed restriction is manageable given [[Emergence Zone]] access. Enters-tapped-without-basics downside is real but ceiling justifies the slot.
