# Memory

Jackson is correcting [[Mentat]]'s hallucinations about Magic: The Gathering card mechanics in his [[Lumra cEDH deck]]. Blossoming Tortoise does NOT untap Lotus Field or tutor it — it mills 3 on ETB/attack, returns a land from graveyard tapped, and reduces land ability activation costs by {1}. Famished Worldsire is 8 mana (5GGG), devours lands for +1/+1 counters, and on ETB looks at top X cards (X = power) to put land cards into play tapped — not a token maker. Jackson is systematically catching card-text errors and asking Mentat to clarify the intended role/synergy of questioned cards before proceeding with deck analysis.
