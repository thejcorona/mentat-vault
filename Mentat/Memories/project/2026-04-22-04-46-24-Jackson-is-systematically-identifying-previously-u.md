# Memory

Jackson is systematically identifying previously undocumented combo lines in his [[Lumra cEDH]] deck by mapping tutors + untappers/bounce outlets. Candidate lines under exploration: (1) Azusa + sol land + untapper for consistent mana generation without requiring Lumra cast/kill cycle; (2) Arboreal Grazer + Lotus Field + untapper for early non-deterministic value; (3) Crop Rotation/Expedition Map + Deserted Temple/Arid Archway loops creating land-bounce engines; (4) Elvish Reclaimer + sacrifice outlets for graveyard acceleration; (5) Burgeoning + high land density + landfall effects for reliable value across opponent turns. Jackson needs direction on which lines are real vs dead ends before mapping them into combo core.
