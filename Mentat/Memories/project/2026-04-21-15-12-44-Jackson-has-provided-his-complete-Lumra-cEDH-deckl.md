# Memory

Jackson has provided his complete [[Lumra]] cEDH decklist (99 cards from Aftermath Analyst through Zuran Orb) to Mentat multiple times. The decklist verification task requires Mentat to pull every single card from [[Scryfall]] systematically, verify mechanics and alternate set names (especially Final Fantasy crossover cards like Ba Sing Se, Balamb Garden, Fal'Cie Paradise), and map each card to its intended synergy/combo line organized by win condition — without asking for re-clarification.
