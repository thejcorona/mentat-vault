# Memory

Ba Sing Se is a confirmed keeper in the [[Lumra cEDH deck]]. Functions as a legitimate combo piece via two lines: (1) Ashaya + Lumra + untapper + sac outlet loop where Earthbend targets Lumra as a land, she becomes 0/0 with 2 counters, dies to sac outlet, Earthbend returns her tapped, Lumra ETB fires, untapper untaps her, repeat infinitely without commander tax; (2) Earthbend a mana-producing land + Springheart Nantuko + Lotus Cobra generates infinite landfall triggers and mana. Sorcery-speed restriction manageable via Emergence Zone. Enters-tapped-without-basics downside is real but ceiling justifies the slot.
