# Memory

Jackson's systematic approach to card evaluation in Lumra: prioritizes understanding INTENDED ROLE (specific synergy, combo piece, interaction mode, ramp payoff) over generic 'is this card good?' assessment. Demands clarity on whether a card is core (required for primary combo), flex (situational utility), or questionable (low-impact include replaceable with stronger alternative). This reflects his broader pattern of proper calibration and strategic thinking before accepting card inclusion in competitive deck.
